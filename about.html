Hello,

  Hello, I'm Jyothish S L, a dedicated IT enthusiast currently pursuing a Master's in Computer Application at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amritapuri. My educational journey includes a BSc in Computer Science from the University of Kerala. I completed my Higher Secondary education at Vivekananda Higher Secondary, Poredom, and my Senior Secondary education at Mar Baselios Senior Secondary School.

  I possess a diverse set of programming skills, including proficiency in C, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and Java. My expertise extends to databases such as MySQL and Oracle, as well as operating systems like Windows, Linux, and Android. I am familiar with frameworks such as PHP Smarty and Laravel, and I have hands-on experience with software like Filmora and MS Office. In addition to my academic achievements, I have actively pursued certifications to enhance my skill set. I have completed Cyber Threat Management and Python essentials courses offered by CISCO online. I also hold certificates in Cyber Security introduction and the basics of MS Office. Additionally, I have completed an Addon Course in Computer Science offered by the PG Department of Mar Thoma College of Science & Technology. I have successfully contributed to various projects, showcasing my practical skills. Notable among them are the development of an online pension payment portal using Smarty for KSRTC PENSION FORUM, the implementation of Digital Edu for online education, and the creation of an Ecommercial web application, Eshopify, using Laravel. I also played a crucial role in developing an Automated Smart Mental Health Helpline (SMH) and an innovative solution for remote patient care and communication called Noosi_TeleConnect, involving ESP32 and Laravel integration. Outside the realm of technology, my interests include web development, content creation, and drawing & craftworking. I have actively participated as a volunteer in the National Service Scheme (NSS) during my degree program, contributing to society while honing my leadership and teamwork skills. Additionally, I received the NCC B Certificate during my service in the National Cadet Corps (NCC), further showcasing my commitment to community service and organizational growth. I am passionate about forging a successful career in IT and am eager to contribute my skills and knowledge to innovative companies.
